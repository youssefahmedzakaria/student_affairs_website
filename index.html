Welcome to our student management website! Our platform is designed to make it easy for users to manage student information, with features including adding, updating, and deleting student records, as well as searching for active students by name and assigning departments to specific students. Our intuitive interface is designed to make these tasks as straightforward and user-friendly as possible, with clear prompts and guidance at every step. In addition, our platform allows you to view all active and inactive students in a separate page, with the ability to change their status with just a few clicks. With a well-designed navigation bar and a clear and informative home page, we're committed to providing you with the tools you need to manage student records with confidence and ease.
